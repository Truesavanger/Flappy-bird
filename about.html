About Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird is a mobile game developed by Vietnamese game developer Dong Nguyen in 2013. It quickly became a viral sensation, thanks to its simple yet addictive gameplay and its challenging difficulty level. The game involves tapping the screen to make a bird flap its wings and navigate through a series of obstacles in the form of green pipes. The goal is to get the bird as far as possible without touching any of the obstacles.
